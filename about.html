    About MedPlus

    MedPlus is a pharmacy retailer that offers a wide range of prescription and over-the-counter medicines. Established in 2006 by Madhukar Gangadi, MedPlus has grown significantly and expanded its presence across various cities and towns in India. It operates both offline retail stores and an online pharmacy platform, offering a variety of healthcare products and services.
